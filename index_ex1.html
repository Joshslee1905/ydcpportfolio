Problem Statement

In 2020, the world's first nocturnal zoo, the Night Safari in Singapore, attracted approximately 343 thousand visitors.
The Night Safari is part of the four wildlife-centered leisure attractions in Singapore, which includes the:

  • Singapore Zoo
  • Jurong Bird Park
  • River Safari

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Night Safari was closed from April to June 2020, and has since
then operated at limited capacity under safety measures.

The following table shows the number of visitors to Night Safari from 2011 to 2020.

Year No. of visitors (in thousands)
2011 1098.5
2012 1163.4
2013 1168
2014 1179.8
2015 1159
2016 1150.5
2017 1261.8
2018 1329.4
2019 1318
2020 343.2
